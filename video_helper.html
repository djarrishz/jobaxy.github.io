How to make your Video resume crisp?
Plan your thoughts and line of action.
Write and rewrite your script until you are satisfied.
Make sure the place where you will record is well lit and away from the background noise.
Keep your script in front of you for guidance but do not directly read from it.
Be confident, look into the camera with a pleasing smile. Talk slowly but confidently in a clear voice.
Make sure your video is clear and audible.
Shoot several takes if necessary before uploading your final video.
Watch your own video several times. Be your own critic.
