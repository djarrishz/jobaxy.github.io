How to Answer?

Sometimes this question can also be asked like â€œWhat were your expectations from your previous company/job?

This question allows employers to know what expectations you have or had from the previous job or organization and how reasonable and realistic is/was it. Also how well they were met in your previous job or how well it can be met in the job that you are applying for. They also want to know how you will be a good fit for the position.

There is no one definite answer to this. Neither there is any right or wrong answer. You should always be professional and honest while answering any of your interview questions and this question is no exception. Set a realistic expectation from your job/company. The expectations should always be in the best interest of you and the company alike.

Sample Answer 1

I expect to be a value-add to the company by using my skills and talents to the full potential. I also expect to learn new things on the job so that I can grow professionally and improve my career graph.

Sample Answer 2

I am a person who is constantly looking for improving my work performance by learning new skills or doing some courses so I can serve my organization in a much better way. I expect my organization will give me ample of chances to enhance my skill set.
